# ROSE TaroWorks - User Guide for Field Workers

## Getting Started

Welcome to ROSE TaroWorks! This guide will help you use the mobile app to complete your field work.

## Logging In

1. Open your mobile browser
2. Go to your organization's ROSE Field App URL
3. Enter your username and password
4. You'll see your dashboard

## Understanding the Dashboard

### My Jobs

Shows all jobs assigned to you:
- **New**: Not started yet
- **In Progress**: You've started working on it
- **Completed**: All tasks finished
- **Synced**: Uploaded to the server

### Sync Status

Shows:
- **Online/Offline**: Your connection status
- **Last Sync**: When you last synced data
- **Pending**: Items waiting to upload

## Working Offline

The app works even without internet! 

**When offline:**
- View assigned jobs and tasks
- Complete forms
- Take photos
- Capture GPS coordinates

**All data saves locally** and uploads when you're back online.

## Completing a Job

### Step 1: Select a Job

1. Tap a job from **My Jobs** list
2. See job details and tasks

### Step 2: Complete Tasks in Order

Tasks must be completed in sequence order:

#### View Data Tasks
- Read the information shown
- Take note of any important details
- Tap **Mark Complete**

#### View Resource Tasks
- View the document/image
- Follow any instructions
- Tap **Mark Complete**

#### Collect Data Tasks
1. Tap the task to open the form
2. Fill in all required fields (marked with *)
3. Take photos if requested
4. Capture GPS location if requested
5. Review your answers
6. Tap **Submit**

### Step 3: Sync Your Data

1. Go to **Sync Status**
2. Tap **Sync Now** when online
3. Wait for sync to complete
4. Check for any errors

## Taking Photos

1. Tap **Capture Photo**
2. Allow camera access
3. Take photo
4. Review and confirm
5. Photo saves locally until synced

## Capturing GPS

1. Tap **Get GPS**
2. Allow location access
3. GPS coordinates captured automatically
4. Coordinates save with your form

## Understanding Form Fields

- **Text**: Type any text
- **Number**: Numbers only
- **Date**: Pick a date from calendar
- **Picklist**: Select from dropdown
- **Required (*)**: Must fill to submit

## Syncing Data

### When to Sync

- **Beginning of day**: Get latest jobs
- **After completing forms**: Upload your work
- **Before end of day**: Final upload

### Auto-Sync

The app syncs automatically when:
- You go back online
- You haven't synced in 4 hours

### Manual Sync

1. Tap **Sync Status**
2. Tap **Sync Now**
3. Wait for "Sync Successful" message

## Troubleshooting

### Can't See My Jobs

- Check if you're logged in
- Tap **Refresh** 
- Try syncing

### Form Won't Submit

- Check all required (*) fields are filled
- Verify field formats (numbers, dates)
- Try saving and submitting again

### Photos Not Uploading

- Check you're online
- Sync again
- File size might be too large (max 10MB)

### GPS Not Working

- Enable location in phone settings
- Allow app location access
- Try again when outside/near window

## Tips for Success

✅ **Sync twice daily** - morning and evening
✅ **Complete tasks in order** - don't skip ahead
✅ **Review before submitting** - check your answers
✅ **Take clear photos** - ensure good lighting
✅ **Note any issues** - report problems to supervisor
✅ **Keep phone charged** - bring power bank
✅ **Save battery** - close unused apps

## Getting Help

If you have problems:
1. Check this guide first
2. Ask your supervisor
3. Contact technical support

**Remember**: All your work saves locally, so you won't lose data even if something goes wrong!
